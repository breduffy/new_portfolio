Art City

Art city is an art finder that allows users to upload interesting art in their city or search for art within a particular city. Art City is a work in progress. Users can filter art by a category. Version two will include Google Maps API for walking tours.

Note: Please allow enough time for heroku to start up before searching for art. For now it takes time.

Monster Maps

Monster Maps is a fun experiment in several techniques I wanted to learn, and thus makes very little sense. Current functionality allows the user to draw a background, drag and drop monster parts, and save their monster in a new window. The user can also place a marker on the map. V2 will allow the monster to be saved at the location.

Note: There are small issues with the drag and drop section and the map area, which I am in the process of fixing.
